B.Pharm Recommended Books (AKTU)

Semester 1

  • Human Anatomy & Physiology I: Essentials of Medical Physiology – Sembulingam
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis I: Pharmaceutical Analysis – Chatwal & Anand
  • Pharmaceutical Inorganic Chemistry: Inorganic Pharmaceutical Chemistry – Gundu Rao; Bentley & Driver
  • Remedial Biology: Textbook of Biology – Verma & Agarwal
  • Remedial Mathematics: Higher Engineering Mathematics – B.S. Grewal

Semester 2

  • Human Anatomy & Physiology II: Textbook of Anatomy & Physiology – Ross & Wilson
  • Pharmaceutical Organic Chemistry I: Organic Chemistry – Morrison & Boyd; IL Finar Vol. 1
  • Biochemistry: Lehninger Principles of Biochemistry; Harper’s Biochemistry
  • Pathophysiology: Essentials of Pathophysiology – Carie Huether
  • Computer Applications in Pharmacy: Computer Applications in Pharmacy – Rajarshi Banerjee

Semester 3

  • Pharmaceutical Organic Chemistry II: IL Finar Vol. 2
  • Physical Pharmaceutics I: Physical Pharmacy – Martin
  • Pharmaceutical Microbiology: Prescott’s Microbiology; Pelczar Microbiology
  • Pharmaceutical Engineering: Pharmaceutical Engineering – C.V.S. Subrahmanyam

Semester 4

  • Pharmaceutical Organic Chemistry III: Organic Chemistry – Jerry March
  • Medicinal Chemistry I: Wilson & Gisvold’s Textbook of Organic Medicinal and Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Physical Pharmaceutics II: Physical Pharmacy – Alfred Martin
  • Pharmacology I: Pharmacology – Rang & Dale
  • Pharmacognosy & Phytochemistry I: Pharmacognosy – Trease & Evans

Semester 5

  • Medicinal Chemistry II: Wilson & Gisvold
  • Industrial Pharmacy I: Lachman’s Theory & Practice of Industrial Pharmacy
  • Pharmacology II: Goodman & Gilman’s Pharmacological Basis of Therapeutics
  • Pharmacognosy & Phytochemistry II: Kokate’s Pharmacognosy

Semester 6

  • Medicinal Chemistry III: Burger’s Medicinal Chemistry
  • Pharmacology III: Rang & Dale; KD Tripathi
  • Herbal Drug Technology: Textbook of Pharmacognosy – C.K. Kokate
  • Biopharmaceutics & Pharmacokinetics: Biopharmaceutics & Clinical Pharmacokinetics – Milo Gibaldi
  • Pharmaceutical Biotechnology: Pharmaceutical Biotechnology – S.P. Vyas & Dixit

Semester 7

  • Instrumental Methods of Analysis: Instrumental Methods of Chemical Analysis – Chatwal & Anand
  • Industrial Pharmacy II: Lachman & Lieberman
  • Pharmacy Practice: Clinical Pharmacy & Therapeutics – Roger Walker
  • Novel Drug Delivery System: Novel Drug Delivery System – Y.W. Chien

Semester 8

  • Biostatistics & Research Methodology: Research Methodology – C.R. Kothari
  • Social & Preventive Pharmacy: Park’s Textbook of Preventive & Social Medicine
  • Pharma Marketing Management: Marketing Management – Philip Kotler
  • Pharmaceutical Regulatory Science: Regulatory Affairs – Global Perspective
  • Pharmacovigilance: Pharmacovigilance – Ronald Mann
  • Quality Assurance: Quality Assurance in Pharmaceuticals – Sidney H. Willig
